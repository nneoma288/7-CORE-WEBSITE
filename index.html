WHO ARE THEY

More Than Music

From Seoul to the World Stage

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bulletproof Boy Scouts, debuted on June 13, 2013, under Big Hit Entertainment. What started as a small hip-hop group from South Korea has evolved into the biggest musical act on the planet.

Their music speaks to the struggles of youth, mental health, self-love, and societal pressures — resonating with millions across language barriers. They've shattered records, redefined K-pop, and proved that music truly has no borders.

JOURNEY

Their Story

2013

Debut with "2 Cool 4 Skool"

Seven boys from different walks of life come together under Big Hit Entertainment. The beginning of something extraordinary.

2015

"I Need U" — First Win

The turning point. The Most Beautiful Moment in Life series launches BTS from rookies to rising stars.

2016

Wings — Global Breakthrough

Wings becomes the highest-charting K-pop album on Billboard 200 at the time. BTS spreads their wings.

2018

Love Yourself — Historic #1

"Tear" becomes the first Korean album to reach #1 on Billboard 200. The UN speech inspires millions with "Speak Yourself."

2020

"Dynamite" — Global Explosion

First Korean act to hit #1 on Hot 100. "Dynamite" lights up the world during dark times. Map of the Soul: 7 makes history.

2022

Proof & Chapter 2 Begins

The anthology album "Proof" marks the end of Chapter 1. Each member begins their solo journey.

2023–2025

Solo Eras & Military Service

All 7 members shine individually. Solo albums break records. Serving their country while ARMY waits with purple hearts.

2025+

The Reunion Awaits 💜

When seven become one again... the world will feel the BTS effect once more. The best is yet to come.
